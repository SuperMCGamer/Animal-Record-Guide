MC Recording Guide

Please use Audacity or your preferred DAW to record your microphone sound. Feel free to do retakes until you are satisfied with the quality. Provide a single recording in WAV format. You DON'T need to trim out individual recordings.

For submission, upload your recording to Google Drive, ensure permissions are set properly, and send the recording link to MC via Discord.
